Remotely Controlled Home

The basic idea behind Remotely Controlled Home is to establish a collaboration between home and technology to make daily chaos simpler and easier. The previous version of Remotely Controlled Home was an integrated system employing technologies such as Internet of Things, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Web Services, System on chip and Android application. It successfully achieved the target of a centralised control which was users smartphone.

Remotely Controlled home is an effective, low cost and smart home automation system with all the necessary features. Our home automation has achieved its objective of establishing an advanced newer milestone of comfort and convenience in living standards of people. Now are entire focus will be on making it dynamic, overcome the limitations and implement the future vision ideas proposed in the previous version.
